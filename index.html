Association for Un-Centralized Governance

Tools and methods for un-centralizing governance

Our Purpose is to:

  • Use technology to foster and cultivate individual participation in organizational governance models
  • Promote the adoption of un-centralized models of governance by way of grants, including grants of respective governance tokens

We Foster:

  • A community of members who are passionate about decentralizing governance and DAO practices
  • A working group to facilitate and advocate for sustainable, secure, and replicable protocol activities

We Serve:

  • Projects interested in researching and exploring methods of uncentralized governance
